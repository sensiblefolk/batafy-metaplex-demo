Overview

Batafy is a decentralized community powered barter marketplace built on Solana that enables users to exchange physical & digital goods securely.

Leveraging the power of NFTS, Bata-Cards will enable holders navigate freely through the barter ecosystem, purchase special placed items in the Batafy Marketplace, earn dividends from platform revenues, unlock special benefits exclusive to early adopters etc. the holder.

The exclusive variant of the card available at launch will have special exclusive Royalty features, meaning holders of the first BATA-Cards will receive royalty from liquidity pool, staking pool and from revenues earned by the platform, just by holding the cards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Bata-NFT ("I owe you card")

The Bata-NFT (“I Owe You Card”) is a transactional card that grant holders access to the bata ecosystem, it will be used for exchanging or bartering of items in the marketplace.

What is the value of the BATA-NFT

The value of the Bata-NFT will be determined by the marketplace, the higher the demand for the Bata-NFTs the more valuable it becomes.

What is the utility of the BATA-NFT

The BATA-NFT enables holders to barter items (digital & physical) on our marketplace, it is tradable, members with the Bata-NFT will be able to stake and access loans from lending protocols. The Bata-NFT will be liquid and interchangeable enabling spot buy and sell.

Can I access the marketplace without the BATA-NFT

Yes! everyone can access the Bata marketplace, view and list items for exchange, but cannot perform any transaction without the BATA-NFT card.

How will items listed on the Batafy Marketplace be vetted

The Batafy marketplace will be managed by community moderators who will verify authenticity of items listed for exchange. The moderator assigns a nominal BATA-NFT equivalent value before approving listed items.

What is the Bata Tipping Process

The Bata Tipping process enables fair opportunity for all members to barter high quality items listed on the marketplace before they get scooped up. When an item is listed, vetted and approved, an auction kicks in for 24 hours. BATA-NFT variant cardholders interested in the item bid by staking their card together with a tiny tip, at the end of the auction the bidder with the most tip wins the Auction.
